From playbook to first sales — your action plan

The playbook tells you how the business works. This page turns it into the order you actually do things in: get legal-ready, get your first inventory, make the website something people can really buy, add the new services (charter, Char Dham) without turning the site into a mess, fix the weak spots, then grow.

The whole plan in one line

Get GST-ready → open free bedbank accounts and tie up one DMC per region → put real inclusions and dates on the four packages → add charter & Char Dham as add-ons (not new menu items) → fix the handful of weak pages → run the Instagram + WhatsApp marketing engine to win your first customers → then stack the high-margin extras and start outbound.

Start this week: (1) register the business and get GST, (2) open one bedbank account and message one DMC, (3) post your first 3 Reels, each ending with "link in bio to plan this trip." Then tick items off below — your progress is shared live with your co-founder.
